About StablePay

StablePay is on a mission to empower retailers and consumers with instant, low-cost crypto transactions. We combine secure Android Point-of-Sale terminals with NFC capabilities and a versatile mobile wallet app for iOS and Android. Our wallet supports XRP & RLUSD on XRPL, and will also support native tokens as well as stablecoins (USDC & EURC) on both Solana and Stellar, enabling users to pay at our Point-of-Sale terminals or securely manage and transfer their tokens.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered

How does StablePay work?

StablePay integrates our Android Point-of-Sale devices with a mobile wallet app for real-time, contactless crypto transactions. Retailers can accept payments in supported tokens, while customers enjoy fast, secure checkouts via NFC or QR code.

What cryptocurrencies are supported?

Our system supports XRP & RLUSD on XRPL, and in the near future also native tokens plus stablecoins (USDC & EURC) on both Solana and Stellar. We continually evaluate adding more assets based on stability, liquidity, and user demand.

Do I need a bank account to accept payments?

No bank account is required. All transactions happen on-chain using stablecoins or other supported tokens. You can later convert your crypto to fiat through our integrated on/off-ramp partners if you choose.

Is KYC required to use the Point-of-Sale terminals?

Yes. Completing KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is necessary to operate our Point-of-Sale terminals. This helps us comply with regulations and maintain a secure, trusted environment for all users.

How do I convert crypto to fiat?

For now, we partner with on/off-ramp services like MoonPay to facilitate easy crypto-to-fiat conversions.

How secure are my funds and transactions?

We leverage the security of underlying blockchains (XRPL, soon also Solana and Stellar) for all transactions. Additionally, our mobile wallet app employs robust encryption and secure key management to protect user funds.

How quickly do transactions settle?

Transactions on our supported blockchains typically settle within seconds. This real-time settlement is a major advantage over traditional payment systems, providing immediate fund availability for retailers.

Can I use StablePay if I'm new to crypto?

Absolutely. Our Point-of-Sale terminals are designed to be user-friendly for retailers, and our mobile wallet app simplifies sending, receiving, and storing supported tokens. We also provide documentation and support to help you get started.

Does the mobile wallet support regular crypto transfers?

Yes. Besides paying at our Point-of-Sale terminals, users can send, receive, and store supported tokens directly within the StablePay wallet. It’s an all-in-one solution for everyday crypto management.

What if I need help or encounter an issue?

We offer comprehensive documentation, a dedicated support team, and an active community channel. If you have any questions or run into technical issues, just reach out via our Contact page or support portal.
